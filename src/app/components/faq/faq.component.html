Q. How would I know if my Official Receipt is valid?

A.  Validity period of Official Receipt is five (5) years only. e.g. If the Official Receipt is printed on July 12, 2019, Official Receipt is still acceptable until July 11, 2024.

Q. Where can I submit my Official Receipts?

A.  You may submit your Official Receipts to any MAPFRE Insurance Sales Office near you.

Q. What are the cut-offs and schedules I need to remember?
A.
  1. All Official Receipts in exchange of commission received from previous month shall be submitted to Head Office c/o Commission personnel on or before 1st Monday of the following month.
  2. In the event that Official Receipt was not submitted during the 1st week, agent should submit the pending Official Receipt before Monday of the following week.
  3. Transfer of Commission to agent's ATM account is every Wednesday and Thursday
  4. Disbursement Statement will be sent to agent's registered email every Wednesday and Friday by senders noreply@mapfreinsurance.com.ph; treasuryho@mapfreinsurance.com.ph
  5. Commission to be released is based on the premiums posted as fully paid in the system from Tuesday (prior week) up to Monday (current week).
Q. How will I be able to receive my commissions for my paid accounts?

A.  Commissions for licensed individuals shall only be paid via transfer to their registered BPI account number.

Q. What if I don't have BPI account? Are there other banks available?

A.  None, but MAPFRE can endorse you to open a BPI account without maintaining balance provided you have regular commission being credited to your account. If after six months and no amount has been credited, the bank shall change it to regular account with required ADB. Other terms and conditions are indicated in the BPI endorsement form.

Q. If I have existing BPI account, can I use it?

A.  Yes, all BPI accounts (current or savings) except for BPI Family Savings

Q. What are the requirements in order to receive my commissions regularly?

A.  Aside from having a valid license and duly submitted MOA (Memorandum of Agreement), you must submit your valid BIR Official Receipts (OR) on or before the due date which is indicated in the email advice sent by MAPFRE to your registered email address. Q. Where can I send my queries or concerns with regards to my commission?

A.  You may email your concerns or queries to DCU@mapfreinsurance.com.ph or mrbdagsa@mapfreinsurance.com.ph

Q. What are the possible causes of a negative amount reflected on my disbursement statement

A.  Aside from the the deductions found in the column advance disbursement, which represent the discounts/commission given to clients by the agent. There will be a negative effect on your commission if there is:

  • returned check/bounced check
  • cancellation endorsement
